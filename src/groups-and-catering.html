Groups and Catering

Ice Cream Trailer Catering

We’ll bring our freezers and staff to serve your party, reception, or BBQ guests. Everything is included in the “per person” price.

Option #1: Ice Cream Cater (Ice Cream Cups Only)

  • Includes: 3 – 5 flavors of ice cream (your choice*), Cups, Spoons, Napkins, Full service for 1.5 hr
  • Price: $450 (minimum of 100 people); $4 per person after meeting minimum.**

Option 2#: Ice Cream Sundae Bar (Ice Cream with Toppings)

  • Includes: 3 – 5 flavors of ice cream (your choice*), 3 – 5 toppings (your choice*), Bowls, Spoons, Napkins, Full service for 1.5 hrs
  • Price: $550 (minimum of 100 people); $5 per person after meeting minimum.**

*May choose from standard list provided for that part of the season
**Location may require an additional charge.

Birthday Parties

Price: $175

What that includes…

  • Rental of Party Space for 2 hours
  • Cups of ice cream for 15 children (or 8 adults)
  • A Woodside Creamery Staff member to scoop your ice cream as well as help you host your party. They can assist with set-up, break-down and anything else you may need.
  • Additional ice cream will be added to your total at $3 per child or $4 per adult.

The Space: Separated from general customer seating by a stone wall and gates, the area will comfortably hold 75 people.

  • The Area is approximately 65 × 45 ft
  • The party tent within the area is approx. 40× 20 ft
  • Includes picnic tables, benches, and fold out tables if needed

*We do not provide and form of entertainment or tours of the farm. You are more than welcome to bring decorations, games, or any entertainment that you like! We only ask that you let us know if you have an entertainer or caterer coming so we can make any special arrangements for accommodating them.

*We schedule the party area on a first come, first serve basis all throughout our season. Please call for availability and scheduling.

*We do require a $75 deposit 2 weeks prior to the date of your party. This will hold the space & time you’ve requested. (Deposit will be returned to you in the event of a proper cancellation due to weather).

Any other question can be directed to Mary or Gretchen in our main office at (302) 239-9847.
